Confirmation of Time

Art Collaboration

"Confirmation of time" is a piece produced by the artist Franziska Hatton in collaboration with me for the "If a Tree Falls in the Forest" Exhibition at the Grizedale Forest. The workpiece explores the concepts of entropy using the forest materials to construct an interpretive model of the expanding Universe and our perception of time. 
This is the result of a 9 months long collaboration between artists from The Royal Academy of Arts and Cosmologists from University College London.

Date: November 2018
Collaborators: Franziska Hatton
Category: Cosmology, Arts, Collaborations, Entropy
