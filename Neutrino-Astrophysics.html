Neutrino Astrophysics

Probing neutrino physics with Cosmological Surveys

Using various cosmological data sets, ranging from Cosmic Microwave Background to the largest spectroscopic galaxy survey to date, my interest is to probe and study neutrino physics from a cosmological perspective. Recently, I have lead a study to obtain constraints for the lightest neutrino mass and the sum of neutrino mass species with a combination of particle physics constraints and cosmological probes.

We found the lightest neutrino to be at least 0.086 eV (95% CI) 

In the media:
